The following is a list of books commonly recommended to aspiring and experienced engineering managers. They are not strictly engineering-related books. Many focus on leadership, management, and human behavior. These are not an official endorsement by the project maintainers but are provided as a resource.

<Aside>
  Contributions welcome! Please submit a PR to add books to this list.
</Aside>
